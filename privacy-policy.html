Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Central TX Card Shows ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Sernascalez LLC, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit centraltxcardshows.com or purchase tickets to our events.

Information We Collect

When you purchase a ticket or sign up for updates, we collect:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Ticket type and payment amount
  • SMS opt-in preference

Payment card information is processed securely by Square and is never stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your ticket purchase and send your confirmation
  • To send event updates, reminders, and show day information
  • To notify you about future Central TX Card Shows events
  • To improve our events and services

SMS Communications

If you opt in to SMS updates during checkout, we may send you event reminders and announcements. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with service providers (Square for payments, Supabase for database, Resend for email) solely to operate our services.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at slabpackz@gmail.com.
